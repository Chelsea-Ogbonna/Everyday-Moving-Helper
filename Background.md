Transporting everyday items such as groceries, laundry baskets, backpacks, moving boxes, household supplies, and travel luggage is a common task that can be physically demanding and inconvenient. Individuals often need to carry multiple items at once while navigating crowded environments, stairs, sidewalks, parking lots, airports, or large campuses. Parents traveling with young children may struggle to manage luggage while simultaneously pushing strollers or supervising children. Delivery workers and couriers frequently transport packages of varying sizes throughout the day, increasing physical strain and reducing efficiency. Similarly, older adults and individuals with mobility limitations may find it difficult to move heavy or bulky items independently.

While wheeled carts, luggage systems, and robotic cargo assistants have been developed to reduce carrying effort, most existing products are designed around a fixed cargo space or footprint. As a result, users must adapt their belongings to the device rather than the device adapting to the cargo. Large or irregularly shaped items may not fit within the available storage area, while smaller items may shift during transport due to excess unused space.

Recent advancements in robotic cargo assistance have introduced autonomous and follow-me technologies that improve convenience. Products such as the Gita cargo robot, FOLO-100 Pro, and AMS Follow-Me Robotic Cart can autonomously transport items by following a user or operating through remote control. However, these systems typically rely on fixed compartments or platform sizes, limiting their ability to securely accommodate cargo with different dimensions and shapes. This limitation reduces their usefulness for users whose transportation needs vary from day to day.

To address this gap, there is a need for a cargo transportation platform that combines robotic mobility with physical adaptability. Inspired by the adjustable mechanism used in expandable phone holders, the proposed Everyday Moving Helper is a modular motorized cargo platform capable of changing its footprint to accommodate a wide variety of objects. The platform incorporates an expandable deck and adjustable side supports that can securely hold groceries, luggage, backpacks, laundry baskets, moving boxes, and other household items while remaining compact when not in use.

By enabling a single device to transport many different cargo types, the Everyday Moving Helper increases versatility while reducing the need for multiple specialized transportation solutions. Its combination of adaptability, compact storage, stability, and remote-controlled mobility differentiates it from existing robotic cargo products and addresses an unmet need for a practical everyday transportation assistant for families, travelers, delivery workers, students, and individuals seeking a more convenient way to move belongings.
